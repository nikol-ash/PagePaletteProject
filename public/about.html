About Page Palette

Welcome to Page Palette, your friendly neighborhood book shop! We believe that every reader deserves a palette of choices, from bestsellers to hidden gems, and from childhood favorites to modern classics. Our shop is lovingly curated for book lovers of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to help you find and share books you love. We want to make discovering your next great read a joy, whether you’re searching for inspiration, a perfect gift, or a cozy escape.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for books and community, Page Palette brings together a diverse collection for every reader. We partner with local authors and independent publishers, and our team is always on the lookout for unique finds to share with you.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity & Diversity

We celebrate stories from all walks of life. Our curated collection includes books from diverse authors, cultures, and perspectives, so every reader can find themselves—and someone new—on our shelves.

Community Connection

Page Palette is more than a bookstore—it's a gathering place for book lovers. We host book clubs, author events, and community drives to foster meaningful connections.

Passion for Discovery

We believe in the magic of discovering something unexpected. Our team is dedicated to recommending hidden gems and supporting emerging voices alongside beloved classics.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to sustainable practices, from eco-friendly packaging to supporting publishers that prioritize responsible sourcing and printing methods.

Personalized Service

Every reader is unique. Whether it’s a thoughtful recommendation, a custom order, or just a warm chat, we strive to make every interaction personal and memorable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I request a book not listed in your shop?

Absolutely! Send us your request by email or through our website’s contact form. We’ll do our best to source it for you.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we deliver anywhere in the Philippines. Orders over ₱500 qualify for free shipping!

How can local authors collaborate with you?

We love working with local talent. If you’re an author or publisher, reach out to us at pagepalette@gmail.com with your details and samples of your work.

Contact Us

Have a question, suggestion, or just want to say hello? Reach out to our friendly team at pagepalette@gmail.com or connect with us on social media. We love hearing from fellow book lovers!
